# Privacy Policy for FiguritApp

**Last updated:** May 1, 2026

FiguritApp ("FiguritApp," "we," "us," or "our") is a mobile application created and operated by an individual developer based in Argentina. FiguritApp helps users manage sticker collections, track missing and duplicate stickers, exchange stickers, and access related app features.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the privacy rights that may apply to users in Argentina and worldwide.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal information, contact us at:

**daniloreitanoandrades@gmail.com**

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to operate, secure, improve, and monetize FiguritApp.

### Account Information

When you create or use an account, we may collect:

- Email address used for authentication.
- Display name.
- Avatar URL.
- Profile picture.
- Authentication provider identifiers, such as Google OAuth identifiers.

### Sticker Collection and Exchange Data

To provide the core features of the app, we may collect:

- Sticker collection data, including stickers you own, need, or mark as duplicates.
- Exchange history, including exchange activity and related collection updates.

### Push Notification Data

If you allow push notifications, we may collect:

- Push notification token associated with your device.
- Notification preferences and delivery status when available.

You can disable push notifications through your device settings.

### Purchase and Subscription Information

FiguritApp may offer a paid premium tier. We do not directly collect or store your credit card number, bank account information, or full payment details.

Premium purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple, Google, and/or RevenueCat. We may receive purchase status information, such as whether your account has an active premium subscription, product identifiers, entitlement status, renewal status, and transaction metadata needed to unlock premium features.

### Advertising Information

Users may see ads in FiguritApp. Advertising may be provided through Google AdMob. Depending on your device settings, Google may process advertising identifiers and related information to serve, measure, limit, or personalize ads.

You can manage ad personalization and advertising identifiers through your device settings and Google privacy controls.

### Analytics and App Usage Information

We may collect app usage information to understand how users interact with FiguritApp and to improve the app. This may include:

- Screens viewed.
- Features used.
- Events related to collection management, exchanges, purchases, or app performance.
- Device and app metadata, such as app version, operating system, and approximate region.

### Crash and Error Information

We may collect crash reports and diagnostic information to fix bugs and improve reliability. This may include:

- Crash logs.
- Stack traces.
- Device model and operating system.
- App version.
- Technical metadata related to the error.

We do not intentionally include sensitive personal information in crash reports.
