# FiguritApp Account and Data Deletion

**Last updated:** May 1, 2026

FiguritApp users can request deletion of their account and associated personal data at any time.

## Request Account Deletion

To request deletion, send an email to:

**[daniloreitanoandrades@gmail.com](mailto:daniloreitanoandrades@gmail.com?subject=FiguritApp%20Account%20Deletion%20Request)**

Please include the following information in your email:

- The email address used for your FiguritApp account.
- Your display name, if available.
- A short message saying that you want to delete your FiguritApp account and associated data.

Example:

```text
Subject: FiguritApp Account Deletion Request

Hello,

I want to delete my FiguritApp account and associated data.

Account email: your-email@example.com
Display name: Your Name
```

## What Will Be Deleted

After we verify the request, we will delete or anonymize personal information associated with your account, including:

- Account information, such as email, display name, avatar URL, and profile picture.
- Sticker collection data.
- Exchange history.
- Push notification token.
- App profile data stored in FiguritApp systems.

## What May Be Retained

Some information may be retained if required for legal, security, fraud prevention, accounting, dispute resolution, or operational reasons. This may include:

- Purchase and subscription records handled by Apple, Google, and/or RevenueCat.
- Limited logs needed for security, abuse prevention, or debugging.
- Records required to comply with applicable law.

Where possible, retained information will be minimized or anonymized.

## Timing

We will review deletion requests as soon as reasonably possible. Most requests are processed within 30 days after verification.

If we need additional information to verify your identity or locate your account, we will reply to your email.

## Premium Subscriptions

Deleting your FiguritApp account does not automatically cancel an active subscription managed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

To avoid future charges, cancel your subscription through your Apple App Store or Google Play Store subscription settings.

Después de verificar la solicitud, eliminaremos o anonimizaremos la información personal asociada con tu cuenta, incluyendo información de cuenta, datos de colección de figuritas, historial de intercambios, token de notificaciones push y datos de perfil almacenados en los sistemas de FiguritApp.
